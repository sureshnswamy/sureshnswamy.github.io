What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Mindfulness is simply paying attention on purpose and in a non-judgmental way. It is like upgrading the operating efficiency of our brain and it can be achieved by training meditation. It is to bring back a wandering attention back to where you want to focus. Whereas self-awareness is moment-to-moment emotional experience. It is to understand our own strengths and weaknesses and being able to access our own inner wisdom in any given situation. As one can see both Self-awareness is fundamentally mindfulness, i.e., to be present in the moment and observe. Since mindfulness is trainable self-awareness must also be also be trainable.

Try at least one of the self-awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

I tried both Body Scan and Journaling. Well with body scan yes it is a more relaxing experience. It opened up a way to observe emotions which was something a new to me. Journaling is fun to just write up just like a personal dairy or a blog. But when to read a day after it provides a nice retrospective view and can help you take all necessary actions required for the future to achieve set goals.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not me" section?

This knowledge that emotions are not me is easier to say or to read in words but unless one actually apply it do something or change something so I can sense emotions arising so I can let them go as easily as those arise.
